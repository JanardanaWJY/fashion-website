ABOUT US

We were established since 1990 in Indonesia. We first had one store that is located in Jakarta. Ever since that, men always come to us looking for their daily clothes. However, clothes were not as their daily needs anymore. They wanted to look cool overtime. Therefore, we decided to not consider clothes by only their comfort, but also by their fashion. We decided to make fashion that can be followed globaly. Now, we sell all of men needs for their fashion. There almost are all of current trend of men's fashionwear here. You will be able to reach them out almost all over the world.
